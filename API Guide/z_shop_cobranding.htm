Working with z-Shops Products

With Amazon Web Services 3.0, you're now able to add your z-Shops products to your site or application. You can also co-brand the payment pipeline, so that customers on your site never leave your look and feel as they move through the ordering process.

By using <input type="hidden" name="purchase-navbar" value="[image you want to appear at the top of the page]"> in your code, you can pass Amazon the image that appears at the top of the payment pipeline. To the customer, the transition from your site to the Amazon payment pipeline will be much smoother.

Please note: The z-Shops payment pipeline is not presently enabled for Amazon's associates programming.

Format

To add a z-Shops buy box to your page, use the form shown below:

<form action="http://s1.amazon.com/exec/varzea/dt/cbop/order-checkout/" method="post">
<input type="hidden" name="purchase-navbar" value="[Image you want to appear at the top of the page]">
<input type="hidden" name="purchase-store-name" value="[the human readable name of your store]">
<input type="hidden" name="purchase-return-url" value="[URL back to your storefront]">
<input type="hidden" name="purchase-exchange-id" value="[the Exchange ID for the ZShops/SDP item]"/>
<input type="hidden" name="purchase-quantity" value="[quantity of the products customer wants to order]" size="2">
<input type="hidden" name="purchase-storefront-name" value="[name of the storefront that is selling the product]"/>
<input type="submit" name=”go” value=”go”>
</form>
